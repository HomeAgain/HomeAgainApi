One platform:
Your home connected

Connect any Arduino® sensor, and soon any connected object that provides an API (Nest® for example) to your HomeAgain Hub (currently a Raspberry Pi). Access a potentially unlimited number of apps to monitor your home activity, control your objects and much more!

Connected objects made easy
for developers

Developers, connect to our HomeAgain API and simply build amazing apps! You can access any SmartHome data* and control objects - you care about the software, we care about everything else. We are already developing two apps and we'll let you play with the infinite number of other possibilities. Your app will also be promoted on the HomeAgain Store!

HomeAgain is Open Source

We would like the community to get involved: if you want to know more about the project, do not hesitate to contact us. We are 3 students from Ecole Centrale Paris (the place where VLC was created) in partnership with a small team from Klee Group, a French IT consulting firm.
